Juelich atlas
--------------

The Juelich atlas is a direct copy of FSL's (Version 5.0) Juelich atlas under
`$FSL_DIR/data/atlases/Juelich/Juelich-prob-1mm.nii.gz`.
For further information see http://fsl.fmrib.ox.ac.uk/fsl/fslwiki/Atlases.
